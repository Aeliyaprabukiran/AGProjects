7yaTools Dashboard - Project Features
======================================

Welcome to 7yaTools, a premium utility dashboard. Here's a list of all current features that you can benefit from:

1. JEWELLERY BILLING & CALCULATOR
   Professional Indian-standard billing with accurate wastage and GST logic.
   Fetch live gold pricing (24K/22K) and download high-resolution invoices.

2. PRICE DASHBOARD (MARKET TRENDS)
   Live tracking for Gold (24K/22K), Silver, and USD exchange rates.
   View a 10-day historical gold history table for better price prediction.

3. SMART INTEREST CALCULATOR
   Calculate simple and compound interest easily with a breakdown of interest earned.

4. HOME LOAN EMI CALCULATOR
   Plan your home loan with an interactive EMI calculator, tenure sliders, and total cost breakdown.

5. JSON COMPARE TOOL (DEEP DIFF)
   Perfect for developers! Deep-compare two JSON objects and see added, removed, or changed keys in a neat tree view.

6. IMAGE COMPRESSOR & RESIZER
   Compress PNG, JPG, and WebP images. Use the "Before/After" slider to verify quality before downloading.

7. QR CODE GENERATOR
   Convert text, URLs, and contacts into custom QR codes with premium color themes.

8. INSTAGRAM HASHTAG GENERATOR
   Generate niche-specific hashtags (Tech, Food, Fitness, etc.) and copy them to your clipboard with one tap.

9. BMI CALCULATOR (HEALTH METRICS)
   Track your health with a visual Body Mass Index calculator featuring height/weight sliders.

10. DEVELOPER NEWS HUB
    Stay updated with the latest tech news from multiple top-tier sources.

11. ADVANCED RANDOM PICKER
    Includes an interactive spinner wheel, speed-draw mode, and a multi-team generator.

12. CUSTOMIZABLE DASHBOARD
    The sidebar is fully reorderable! Simply drag and drop with your mouse or finger to move your favorite tools to the top.

13. FULLY RESPONSIVE & DARK MODE
    Works smoothly on Mobile, Tablets, and Desktops. Switch between Light and Dark mode for the perfect viewing experience.
