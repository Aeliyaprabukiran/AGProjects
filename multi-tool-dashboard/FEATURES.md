# 🛠️ 7yaTools Dashboard - Project Features

Welcome to **7yaTools**, a premium, all-in-one utility dashboard built for professionals and casual users alike. This multi-tool platform combines financial calculators, developer utilities, and creative tools into a single, high-performance interface.

---

## 📈 Financial & Market Features
### 1. Jewellery Billing & Calculator
*   **Professional Invoices**: Generate Indian-standard jewellery bills with accurate GST and wastage logic.
*   **Live Pricing**: Automatically fetches real-time gold rates (24K/22K) per gram.
*   **Wastage & Making Charges**: Supports percentage-based, per-gram, or fixed making charges.
*   **One-Click Download**: Export high-resolution invoice images for customers.

### 2. Live Market Dashboard
*   **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor Live Gold, Silver, and USD to INR exchange rates.
*   **Trend Analysis**: View 10-day historical gold rates in a responsive table.
*   **Live Indicators**: Visual cues (green/red) for price trends.

### 3. Smart Interest Calculator
*   **Dual Mode**: Calculate both Simple and Compound interest easily.
*   **Visual Growth**: Real-time breakdown of principal vs. interest earned.

### 4. Home Loan EMI Calculator
*   **Financial Planning**: Calculate monthly EMIs, total interest payable, and total cost of the loan.
*   **Interactive Controls**: Sliders for principal, tenure, and interest rates.

## 💻 Developer & Efficiency Tools
### 5. JSON Compare (Deep Diff)
*   **Precision Comparison**: Detect every change between two JSON objects, including nested objects and arrays.
*   **Tree View Results**: Expandable results highlighting **ADDED**, **REMOVED**, and **MODIFIED** keys.
*   **Strict Mode**: Optionally compare key order for identical-looking objects.

### 6. Image Compressor (Pro Grade)
*   **Multi-Format Support**: Compress PNG, JPG, and WebP images.
*   **Real-Time Comparison**: Use our "Before/After" slider to see quality differences instantly.
*   **Intelligent Resizing**: Maintain aspect ratios while shrinking file sizes up to 90%.

### 7. QR Code Generator
*   **Universal Support**: Generate QR codes for Text, URLs, and vCards (Contacts).
*   **Custom Aesthetics**: Premium color presets, hex-code support, and customizable backgrounds.
*   **Downloadable Assets**: Save QR codes as high-quality PNGs.
